# Industrial Electrical Maintenance

## Objective
This repository focuses on practical electrical maintenance skills required
for factories and industrial environments.

## Learning Scope
- Industrial wiring practice
- Single-phase and three-phase motors
- DOL and Star-Delta starters
- MCB, MCCB, contactor, relay
- Preventive and breakdown maintenance
- Electrical safety during maintenance

## Outcome
- Job-ready industrial electrical skills
- Maintenance and troubleshooting ability
